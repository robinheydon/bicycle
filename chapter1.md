# What is a bicycle

A bicycle is a human-powered machine.

## Understanding a bicycle

### Anatomy of a utility bicycle

* Hub brakes
* Dynamo lights
* Hub gears
* Stand
* Back rack
* Front basket
* Step-through frame
* Swept back handlebars
* Comfortable seat
* Integrated wheel lock
* Full length mud guards
* Skirt / Coat guards
* Fully enclosed chain guard

### Other styles of bicycle

* Mountain
* Road
* Racing
* Touring

### Other types of bicycle

* Cargo
* Tandem
* Unicycle
* Tricycle
* Recumbant
* Wheelchair bicycle
